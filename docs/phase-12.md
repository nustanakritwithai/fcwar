# Phase 12 — Onboarding and HUD

After class selection, a dismissible Thai quick-start explains the six actions
that make the round loop. Class-specific guidance stays visible. Faction score,
capture state, player HP/stamina/gold/level/class, inventory/faction stock,
online count, minimap, hotbar, death location, and victory reset each have one
clear HUD region. Player meshes, labels, and map dots use faction color.
